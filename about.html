Pastor
Victor B. Rountree

Is the Founder and Senior Pastor of Endure To The End Ministries. He has been in ministry for twenty years. He has served as the pastor of Upper Room Church COGIC in Korea. He served as Assistant Pastor for six years to Gospel Outreach Ministries in Grand Forks, ND and Associate Pastor to Grace Covenant in Albuquerque, NM. He served our nation as a 30-year Chief Master Sergeant in the United States Air Force. Pastor Rountree has earned his BS in Accounting from the University of Maryland. He is a father of three and a mentor and pastor to many. In 2017, he retired as the Budget Officer at Offutt AFB, NE.

Co-pastor
Cheryl J. Rountree

She is better known as "Mother Rountree." She serves as Co-Pastor to her husband at Endure To The End Ministries. She is Co-Founder of the ministry. Mother Rountree has served in the ministry for over twenty years and is a true servant of the Most High God. She is the Jurisdictional Supervisor of the North Dakota Jurisdiction, Church of God In Christ, Inc. Mother Rountree is a writer and author, and she has published her first book "A Father of the Fatherless." She has earned a Masters degree from Boston University. She has served as an intercessor for over 30 years praying for the nations.
